During my time at Rugters, I learned to create visualizations that were both meaningful and aesthetically pleasing. I was able to work with data on renewable energy to show its distribution through the country. My group and I designed heatmap, choropleth and location marker graphs through Leaflet, showing both where different types of renewable energy plants are located through the country and which states had the largest amount of renewable energy production. We then utilized JavaScript to design sunburst visualization from the Plotly library to analyze the distribution of energy production within the United States. We complimented this chart with a list, that updates daily, of articles related to the growth of renewable energy. We built a Flask application that navigated through different html files and launched it on Amazon Web Services.





I was also able to experiment with analytical tools. In one partilucar project, my team and I used machine learning models to explore the behavior of two different stocks. The stocks we chose were Cisco Systems, Inc. (CSCO) and New Oriential Education (EDU). Cisco Systems (CSCO) supplies hardware and software for networking solutions and other communication needs like mobility and security. It also provides provides software and security for telemedicine platforms, 5G mobile connections, and platforms for companies to analyze data through AI and machine learning. New Oriental Education & Technology Group (EDU) provides remote educational services to people in China. Both these companies are positioned in industries that are set to grow, but we wanted to see if we could predict their future stock prices using past prices. We used a Long Short-Term Model to predict future stock prices based on past prices. We used a Multi-variate linear regression to anaylze the impact that certain fundementals had on a stock's price.
